About the Challenge

The Centre for Innovation in Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Aviation (IRADA) at Emirates Aviation University is excited to announce EAU AIxplore 2025: IRADA Intra-University Innovation Challenge in AI, Data Science & Roboticsâ€”a prestigious platform for undergraduate students from various disciplines to demonstrate their creativity, problem-solving skills, and technical expertise.

Competition Structure

The competition is structured in two phases:

  • Phase 1: Project Proposal Submission (April 30, 2025) - Submit proposals addressing key challenges in Data Science, AI, and Robotics across various sectors.
  • Phase 2: Final Project Submission & Presentation (October 2, 2025) - Develop and present projects before expert judges.

Topics & Sectors

Participants are encouraged to submit proposals related to:

  • Healthcare: AI diagnostics, predictive analytics, hospital operations
  • Aviation: Flight efficiency, predictive maintenance, air traffic control
  • Smart Cities: Traffic optimization, energy efficiency, public services
  • Cybersecurity: Threat detection, anomaly detection, security automation
  • Education: Personalized learning, automated assessments
  • Finance: Fraud detection, algorithmic trading
  • Energy: Consumption management, renewable optimization
  • Robotics: Industrial automation, intelligent systems

Eligibility

  • Open to all undergraduate students at Emirates Aviation University
  • Teams of up to 2 members
  • Individuals may participate in maximum 2 projects

Support & Resources

Shortlisted participants will receive:

  • Targeted workshops in Python, Excel, Power BI, and MATLAB
  • Mentorship tailored to project requirements
  • Technical resources to strengthen implementation

Awards & Recognition

  • Prizes for top 3 performing teams
  • Certificates of participation for all Phase 2 participants
  • Opportunity to be featured and nominated for top tech competitions
